London Police Department

Working with Londoners to make London safer.

What Do We Do?

We are an umbrella organisation that liases with the communities and police forces of London. We aim to bring London together in resolving and stamping out crime in order to improve the well being of it's citizens

How Do We Do It?

Using this service we have created a service similar to Interpol - where the faces of criminals are advertised on the web for millions of people to see. This in turn acts as a repellent for criminals whilst holding criminals to account.

Can You Help?

If you have any information regarding the people you see, please call 101. If you know of somebody that has committed a crime in the London area, do not hesitate to create an account with us and post to our page.
