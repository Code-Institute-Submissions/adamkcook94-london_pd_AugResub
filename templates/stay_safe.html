Staying safe on the streets of London

Whilst sometimes being a victim of street crime is completely unavoidable, there habits you can develop to limit the risk. Here's the advice that we believe will be beneficial to yourself and allow you to go outside with confidence.

Be Prepared.

Plan your route, make sure your mobile phone is fully charged, and tell someone where youâ€™re going.

Be confident.

Look assertive and walk with confidence. This will always make you appear much less vulnerable and deter criminals.

Hide your valuables.

Keep your valuables including your mobile phone, other devices and jewellery, hidden. If thieves can't see it, they can't steal it.

Trust your instincts.

Do your best to avoid walking alone at night in dark alleyways, parks and side streets or any unfamiliar environment. If you do have to walk, stick to busy places and areas with good lighting.
